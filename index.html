First Public Offering in Napeague Camping Club History

For what may be the first time in the history of the Napeague Camping Club, a residence within this private waterfront community is being offered to the public market.

Located at 31 Harbor Road in Amagansett, New York, this modern three-bedroom, two-bathroom residence presents a rare opportunity to acquire a home in one of the most unique and least understood communities in the Hamptons.

Napeague is literally one of the least populated and least developed parts of the Hamptons, sitting on a narrow strip of land between the Atlantic Ocean and Gardiners Bay. The area is known as a kiteboarding and windsurfing destination, and has evolved from a 1950s campground into a unique residential community of working waterfront families.
